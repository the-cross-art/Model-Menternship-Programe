Timeline

  • Day1:A Group Discussion will be organized to lay emphasis on Verbal Communication, Non-Verbal Communication and Listening Skills.
  • Day2: JGroup Planning task will be conducted for imparting Problem Solving and Decision-Making skills
  • Day3:An assessment will be given to mark the significance of Assertive behaviour.
  • Day4: A Google Meet session will be conducted to discuss the aforementioned assessment and the qualities of Professionalism.
  • Day5: A Google Meet session laying stress on Team Work will be conducted. Before the meet, students will be divided into groups by mentors
  • Day6: A short doubt session regarding the project will be organised.
  • Day7:The respective teams will present their presentation within 10 minutes (max). Also they will be submitting a short summarised report/experience of the entire programme.
